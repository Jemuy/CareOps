CareOps AI is an Operational Assurance and Inspection Readiness Platform for Ofsted-regulated Children's Homes and Supported Accommodation Services.
The platform continuously evaluates whether a service is inspection-ready and identifies evidence gaps, compliance risks, safeguarding concerns, workforce issues, governance weaknesses, and documentation deficiencies before Ofsted identifies them.
Core Problem it aims to resolve: Registered Managers and Responsible Individuals often discover inspection weaknesses too late.
Evidence exists across multiple systems but is: Fragmented, Incomplete, Difficult to evidence, Poorly monitored, Not linked to regulatory expectations. CareOps AI solves this by continuously monitoring readiness against Ofsted expectations.
