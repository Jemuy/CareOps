Product Vision

CareOps AI is an Operational Assurance and Inspection Readiness Platform for Ofsted-regulated Children’s Homes and Supported Accommodation Services.

The platform continuously evaluates whether a service is inspection-ready and identifies evidence gaps, compliance risks, safeguarding concerns, workforce issues, governance weaknesses, and documentation deficiencies before Ofsted identifies them.

The platform is NOT primarily a care recording system.

The platform sits above existing systems such as:

* Log My Care
* ClearCare
* CHARMS
* Access
* Internal spreadsheets
* HR systems

and acts as a continuous inspection-readiness and operational assurance layer.

⸻

CORE PROBLEM

Registered Managers and Responsible Individuals often discover inspection weaknesses too late.

Evidence exists across multiple systems but is:

* Fragmented
* Incomplete
* Difficult to evidence
* Poorly monitored
* Not linked to regulatory expectations

CareOps AI solves this by continuously monitoring readiness against Ofsted expectations.
